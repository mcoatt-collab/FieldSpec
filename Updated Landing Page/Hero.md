# FieldSpec – Hero Section Prompt

## Objective

Design a modern AI-style hero section that clearly communicates FieldSpec’s value: turning field images into structured reports.

## Layout

* Left:

  * Headline
  * Subtext
  * CTA buttons
  * Trust line

* Right:

  * Product UI preview (report or dashboard mock)

## Content

Headline:
Turn Field Images into Structured Reports — Instantly

Subtext:
Upload inspection images. FieldSpec analyzes them and generates stakeholder-ready reports in minutes.

CTA:

* Get Started (primary)
* View Sample Report (secondary)

Trust Line:
No credit card required

## Trust Strip (below hero)

* “Generate reports 5x faster”
  OR
* “Built for modern field inspection workflows”

* Minimal
* Strong typography hierarchy
* Subtle gradients (Linear-inspired)
* Clean spacing (Stripe-inspired)
