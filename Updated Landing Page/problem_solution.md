# FieldSpec – Problem & Solution Section Prompt

## Objective

Communicate the pain of manual inspection workflows and how FieldSpec solves it.

## Layout

  * Left: Problem
  * Right: Solution

* Below:

## Content

Problem:
Manual inspection reporting is slow and inconsistent

* Images are unstructured
* Findings are written manually
* Reports take hours to compile

Solution:
FieldSpec automates the workflow

* AI analyzes images instantly
* Findings are structured automatically
* Reports are generated and ready to export
